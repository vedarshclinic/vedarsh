Solanki

MA Psychology (VNSGU) DCCP (Guj. Uni.) Ph.D. (Suicidal Tendency, Rajkot Uni.) Certified Yog Trainer (GSYB) Child Development Counselling & Therapy

Dr. Renuka Solanki is a highly respected Consultant Psychologist and Therapist with over 13 years of dedicated practice. She specializes in child development, adolescent psychology, family counselling, and therapeutic interventions rooted in evidence-based methodologies.

Her approach blends the richness of ancient Indian wisdom with contemporary psychological science, creating a uniquely holistic environment at Vedarsh — a sanctuary for genuine inner transformation.
